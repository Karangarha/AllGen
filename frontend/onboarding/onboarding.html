Why this matters

Over 32 million Americans live with food allergies. Online grocery sites often hide ingredients in images or bury them deep in product pages — making allergens easy to miss.

AllergenGuard scans every product page automatically and warns you the moment it spots one of your allergens.

⚡

How it works

AllergenGuard works automatically on the most popular food and grocery sites. When it finds one of your allergens, a red banner slides down at the top of the page.
